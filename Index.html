About Merge

Merge is an innovative ed-tech platform designed to help students navigate the world of open-source contributions and ace prestigious programs like Google Summer of Code (GSoC), MLH Fellowship, and more. Founded with the mission to bridge the gap between students and experienced mentors, we provide personalized guidance, valuable resources, and a community-driven environment for learning and growth.

Our team of highly experienced mentors brings years of expertise in software development and open-source projects. At Merge, we believe that with the right support, every student can succeed in making meaningful contributions to the open-source ecosystem, gaining invaluable experience and skills.

Whether you're just starting out or already have experience, Merge offers tailored mentorship programs to help you grow and excel. We are committed to creating an inclusive and collaborative learning space where students can thrive and make impactful contributions to the tech community.
